Why go to grad school? What can I do with a masters or a PhD degree in CS? Learn the answers to these questions and many others at this event, specifically targeting undergraduates in their 4th and 3rd years. A panel of CS alumni, graduate students, and faculty will share their experience on why they choose to pursue a MSc or a PhD in Computer Science, their career paths after graduation, and how to apply to graduate school in UBC and other universities.
